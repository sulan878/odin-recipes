Cake

Description

a sweet baked food made from a dough or thick batter usually containing flour and sugar and often shortening, eggs, and a raising agent

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks butter
  • 3 cups flower
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. whisk flower, baking powder, and salt in a bowl until combined
  3. divide the batter between prepared pans and bake until lightly golden
